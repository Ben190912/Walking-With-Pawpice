Walking With Pawpice is a website dedicated to helping people find suitable dog-friendly walks in the greater Hawke's Bay area.

There are plenty of scenic and beautiful areas in our region, but not all of them allow dogs, and some may be too difficult for the average dog. Our website allows you to browse by area, difficulty or search on a map so you can plan an outing with your furry friend.

Walking with Pawpice is a small student project developed by Ben Mason for the BCS degree at EIT. The site is in a state of ongoing development, and features may be subject to change as the site reaches maturity. The site is for educational purposes only. The site is a showcase for the skills I'm learning in Website development. This site complies with the NZ Privacy Act 2020.
