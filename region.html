Dog Walks in the wider HB Region:

Puketapu Park

Puketapu Park is a free to use regional park in Puketapu. It features a large grassy area, and a playground. The whole park is an off-leash area, however care must be taken around kids in the playground. NOTE: this link does not work, and just for display purposes.

Haumoana Beach

Haumoana Beach is a free to access stony beach. The entire length of the beach is an off-leash area. Care must be taken to supervise your dog around the waves, incase they are swept out to sea. NOTE: this link does not work, and just for display purposes.

Te Mata Peak Walking Tracks

Te Mata Peak Walking tracks are a series of walking tracks on the city side of Te Mata Peak. There are differing tracks for all skill levels however, there are only leashed areas available here. NOTE: this link does not work, and just for display purposes.
