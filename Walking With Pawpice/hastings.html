Hastings Dog Walks:

Pakowhai Regional Park

Pakowhai Regional Park is a dedicated dog walking park with agility areas, grassy fields, and water. Care must be taken with dogs off-leash as many dogs and owners frequent the park. There are both leashed and off-leash areas. NOTE: this link does not work, and just for display purposes.

Cornwall Park

Cornwall Park is a free to use city park with an open field and walking track. There are both leashed and off-leash areas. NOTE: this link does not work, and just for display purposes.

Hawkes Bay Regional Park

Hawkes Bay Regional Park is a dedicated sports field with some walking tracks available for dogs. There are only leashed areas available here. NOTE: this link does not work, and just for display purposes.
