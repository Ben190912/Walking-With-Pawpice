Napier Dog Walks:

Anderson Park

Anderson Park is a free to use city park with an open field and a walking track. There are both leashed and off-leash areas. There is local wildlife so caution must be taken with your dog off-leash.

Park Island

Park Island is a free to use city park with an open field and walking track. There are both leashed and off-leash areas. NOTE: this link does not work, and just for display purposes.

Marine Parade

Marine parade is a centrally positioned beach in the heart of Napier CBD. It has grassey and stone areas, with a walking track. There are both leashed and off-leash areas. NOTE: this link does not work, and just for display purposes.
