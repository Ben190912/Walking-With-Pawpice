Anderson Park - Napier

Length: Up to 2.25km

Difficulty: Easy

Features: Large Field, Pond

Cautions: Wildlife

Off-leash areas - Leashed areas

Anderson Park is a free to access, in-city park with a full paved walking track and a large grass area. It features many drinking fountains, partial tree coverage, and an urban setting. It is frequented by many dog walkers, and hosts events during the summer. It is surrounded by road and entry points, so care is recommended around the edges of the park.
